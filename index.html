What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security and is typically decentralized, meaning it's not controlled by any central authority.

Examples of cryptocurrencies include Bitcoin, Ethereum, Ripple, and Litecoin.
